About Us

Fast Food Wiz is a website started by a college student who was tired of having to search so many different places to figure out what the best deals are. He figured if he has this problem then many other people probably do as well. He created this website so that you can find all of the best deals from your favorite fast food restaurants in one simple place. Here you can enter your budget and find all of the best deals for you. You can also see what other people's favorite deals are on the community page, where you can also sign up and share your favorite deals.
